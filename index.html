About us


Mint Roofing and Construction LLC is a Licensed & insured locally owned roofing and construction business with over 70 years of experience. We are a family owned business in Florida ready to meet your needs anytime. Mint Roofing and Construction offers top-notch residential and commercial roofing services around the area. We specialize in roof repairs, installation, and replacement. The types of roofs we do are Asphalt, Tile, and Sheet metal. Our team is dedicated to providing reliable, quality services with a focus on customer satisfaction. With efficient and timely communications and proper installation techniques.
